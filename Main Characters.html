Main Characters

  • SpongeBob SquarePants

    SpongeBob SquarePants is one of the main characters in the series of the same name. He is a yellow, square sea sponge, wearing brown shorts, a white shirt, a red tie, and black shoes. He lives in a pineapple house in the fictional city of Bikini Bottom and works as the head chef at the famous fast-food restaurant, the Krusty Krab. He is Mr. Krabbs' best employee. His pet is a snail named Gary, and his best friend is Patrick Star.


    His hobbies include jellyfishing, blowing bubbles, and practicing karate.

  • Patrick Star

    Patrick is a pink starfish, SpongeBob's best friend, and one of Squidward's least favorite characters. He wears purple flowery beach shorts and often gets into humorous situations with SpongeBob. He has no regular job but is a loyal customer of the Krusty Krab and sometimes works there or at the Chum Bucket as a temporary employee.


    His hobbies include daydreaming, sleeping, and eating lots of ice cream.

  • Gary the Snail

    Gary, whose full name is Garold "Gary" Wilson Snail, Jr., is SpongeBob's pet snail. He can understand and communicate in natural language and mostly stays at SpongeBob's house. He is very intelligent and leaves a trail of slime wherever he goes.


    It is said that he is cousins with Patrick Star, according to some royal family lineage.

  • Mr. Krabs

    Mr. Krabs is a red crab, whose full name is Eugene Krabs. In the Navy, he was known as "Armor Abs." He is obsessed with money and is very cheap (even with his own daughter), but sometimes he treats SpongeBob to a Krabby Patty. He has a daughter named Pearl, a sperm whale. His father, Redbeard, was a pirate. He once went on a date with Auntie Puff.


    Due to his greed and stinginess, he is often portrayed as an anti-hero in the show.

  • Plankton

    Plankton is a tiny plankton, a villainous character in the series, and the arch-nemesis of Mr. Krabs. He has one eye and is known for being malicious and devious.


    He is known for his evil plans and attempts to steal the Krabby Patty secret formula.

  • Squidward Tentacles

    Squidward is a blue-ringed octopus with a big nose. He is often grumpy and vain, thinking of himself as an artistic genius. He is bald but used to have long, yellow hair, which he wishes to grow back.


    He lives in a house shaped like an Easter Island statue, with SpongeBob and Patrick as his neighbors.


    Squidward is also a talented cook.
